What is Water Pollution?

Water pollution is the contamination of water bodies such as rivers, lakes, and oceans due to harmful substances. It has devastating effects on ecosystems and human health.

Key Statistics

  • Over 2 million people die each year due to water-related diseases.
  • More than 80% of the world’s wastewater is released into the environment without treatment.
  • 1 in 3 people worldwide lack access to safe drinking water.
